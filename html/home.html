Benefits

Who doesn't want someone to help them navigate all the tricky moments in their life?
But finding the perfect mentor isn't as easy as just asking someone you admire to give you advice, is it? SibLinks has the answer.

Get instant help from the most high-achieving college students for not only academic subjects, but also for help with their social lives.
The mentors are students from MIT, Harvard and Stanford, playing the roles of siblings in college reaching back to encourage and support high school students.

SibLinks is the first networking environment in which high school students will have access to the most high-achieving college students.
SibLinks will connects you with experts for live help via screen sharing, video, and text chat.

SibLinks is not just the platform that will connect high school students to a crowd of mentors.
It's your siblinks always with you to solve your problems.
